Normalize inspection and thermal findings into a canonical schema.

Rules:
- Do not invent facts not present in the source finding.
- Preserve the meaning of the original observation.
- Use client-friendly area labels where possible.
- If the area cannot be inferred, return "Not Available".
- Normalize synonymous terms such as dampness, seepage, leakage, moisture ingress, paint damage, tile hollowness, and thermal anomaly.
- Keep normalized observations short, clear, and usable across similar inspection reports.
